Day 1–3: Fix the Algorithm Fix
⌄
Strategy
  • Stop the "Documentary": Cut the long business talk. Only show the top 3 most painful problems your clients face. Start with "Stop doing [X] if you want [Y]." (e.g., Stop doing generic editing if you want more retention).
  • Create the "Before/After": Make a Reel showing "Raw Footage vs Final Video" with a fast-speed transition in the middle.
  • The "Glow Up": Post a side-by-side of your editing workflow (Day 1 — messy) vs Day 30 (clean). Text overlay: "How I cleaned up my process."
The Plan
  • Day 1: Post "Boring Editing is Killing Your Views" (Documentary style but fast).
  • Day 2: Post "Before vs After" of a client project (Speed up the footage).
  • Day 3: Post "The Mistakes I Made in 4 Years" (Personal/Vulnerable).
Insight: When you post "How I edit," nobody cares. When you post "Why your edit looks cheap," people stop scrolling immediately.

Day 13–20: The Reel-to-Lead Growth
⌄
Format: The "Pain Agony"
  • Identify your clients' biggest fear (e.g., "You have footage but no one is watching").
  • Create a 7-second video where you say this. Visual: Text on screen shaking or a sad emoji, then boom — you have the solution.
  • CTA (Crucial): "Comment 'FIX' and I'll send you the strategy." Do not ask them to DM. DMs are spammy. Comments are tracked leads.
Format: The "Sneak Peek"
  • Take a finished video you just delivered.
  • Post a "Behind the Scenes" of the editing process for the first 15 seconds.
  • CTA: "DM me 'EDIT' to see what I did."
Outreach / DMs
  • Reply to the "FIX" comments immediately (within 15 mins).
  • Send an automated Loom video (2 mins max) analyzing their current brand.
Insight: Don't lead with "I edit for $X". Lead with their pain. "I help e-com brands increase AOV" is better than "I use Premiere Pro."
Day 21–30: The Offer Close
⌄
The 24-Hour Reel
  • Record a real client call. Don't script it. Ask them: "What was the biggest result you got?" and "What was the hardest part?" This builds massive trust.
  • Case Study: Post a simple slide deck: Problem → My Edit → Result (e.g., "Views went from 200 to 50k").
